Premium Boat Rental Services

Fireworks

Starting from Memorial Day Weekend until Labor Day Weekend, there are fireworks displays at Navy Pier. You can book a private charter to enjoy a summer evening with stunning sunsets and the fireworks display at Navy Pier.

Holidays

Make holidays and weekends extra special with specialty prices and limited availability for occasions like Fourth of July Weekend, Labor Day Weekend, Memorial Day Weekend, Chicago Scene Boat Party, and Chicago Air & Water Show.

Celebration Events

Whether planning a birthday, bachelor/bachelorette party, or graduation celebration, a private boat charter is a perfect way to make the event unforgettable.

Corporate Outings

Treat your team to a unique and memorable experience by chartering a boat for your next corporate outing. The luxury boats offer a stunning setting for team-building events, client meetings, or employee appreciation parties.

Social Gatherings

Make your next social gathering unforgettable by chartering a boat. Enjoy stunning views of the water and city skyline while socializing with your guests.
